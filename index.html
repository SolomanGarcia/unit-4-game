Crystal Collector

You will be given a random target number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on the crystals you will add aspecic amount of points to your current score.
You win the game by matching your current score to the target score. You lose by having the value of your current score going above the target score.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts the values of the crystals and the target score will change.
